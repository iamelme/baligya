Baligya (Cebuano: to sell) is a lightweight, open-source tool designed to help individuals and small sellers keep track of their products and daily transactions.

Think of it as a digital notebook for sellers — simple, flexible, and easy to use.

Baligya focuses on helping users manage their inventory and record sales in a straightforward way, without the complexity of traditional systems.

It is built for real-world use where simplicity and accessibility matter most.

This system includes:

Features

* Product listing and basic inventory tracking

* Simple sales logging

* Transaction history (informal records)

* Price checking / quick reference

* Item movement tracking (stock in/out)

Disclaimer

Baligya is not a Point of Sale (POS) system.

It does not:

Issue official receipts
Perform tax calculations
Integrate with government or regulatory systems

This tool is intended for informal tracking and personal record-keeping only.


Baligya aims to empower local communities by giving them tools to manage their small businesses without barriers, fees, or complicated requirements.
